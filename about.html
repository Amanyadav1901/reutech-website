ABOUT THE COMPANY

REUTECH INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED is part of South African Publicly listed group company named REUNERT LIMITED, which was established in 1888 and manages a portfolio of businesses in the fields of Electrical Engineering, Information Communication Technologies (ICT) and Applied Electronics.

REUTECH India Private Limited is a domestic private limited company, incorporated on 11thjan,2019, having its registered office located at S3, First Floor, Green Park Extension, New Delhi 110016.

The establishment of REUTECH India Private Limited re-enforces the long standing commitment to grow and support its customers for the "Make in India" initiative. The company has already established key strategic supply chain parternships and is in the process of forming JV partnerships for the transfer of technology and know how to meet the stringent indigenization and local content requirements.
