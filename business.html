BUSINESS

The Business objective for RIPL is to generate profit and increase the wealth of its shareholders as well as group company by providing sales and service support in India to all the the divisions of the group company which are engaged in manufacturing and distribution of high tech Electonics, Communication and Surveillance products for defence as well as commercial use.

The establishment of RIPL enforces the group company's long standing commitment to grow and support the business under "Make in India" initiative.

Providing the following services to all the divisions of the group company-

Marketing, Advertising, Servicing, Sales support services, sub-contract manufacturing, procuring, sourcing, supplying, distributing, importing, exporting and reselling etc.
