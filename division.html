REUTECH COMMUNICATIONS

A division of Reutech (pty) Limited and Owned by Reunert Limited (JSE listed company), Reutech Communication has been a key supplier to the South African National Defence Force(SANDF) for over 50 years. The Company is also an established supplier to manyinternational customers and OEM's.

Reutech Communications specialises in adavanced Secure military communication systems for Airborne, Naval and Land based applications in the HF, VHF and UHF frequency bands.

PRODUCTS

Tactical radios

The tactical ground-based family of radios provides communications that can be configured for man-portable, vehicle fit or jerk and run applications.

Also included an intelligent Built in test (BIT), on board GPS for sync and position reports as well as a versatile interface to external applications, Network maintenance and control is done via simple Network Mangement Protocol (SNMP).

The front panel interface is the same for all comfigurations whether man-portable or vehicle fit, thus allowing ease of use in the operation of the radio. Control is via USB and/or Ethernet interfaces.
